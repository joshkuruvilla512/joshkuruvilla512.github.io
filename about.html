About Me

Hi I'm Joshua Kuruvilla! I enjoy travelling, photography, and exploring. All of the below pictures are my own :)

Besides my hobbies, I am also involved in work oversees. My father created an organization in 2019 called Marc, (Master Activated Resources for Care). It is a network of people across the globe volunteering to teach key skills like Computer Software, Physics and even Softskills like Presenting, Time Management, In person Networking and creatively thinking and working together.This organization will be mentoring people who cannot afford to go to college. Right now this organization is currently only serving a group of about 75 kids that are in an orphanage in Andhra Pradesh, India!

Soon I will be dropping a new website and app for this organization. This is what I want to use my newfound skills for! If you want to learn more information or want to get involved, please send me an email or connect with me by clicking on my social media icons below!
